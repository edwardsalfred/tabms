Who We Are

About The TAB

Spirit-filled. Community-rooted. D'Iberville proud.

Our Mission

Returning Productive,
Positive-Thinking People
Back to the Community.

At Tabernacle Missionary Baptist Church, we believe that the church exists not just for Sunday — it exists for Monday through Saturday as well. Our mission is to equip every member to carry their faith into every corner of the community.

We are a congregation built on sound biblical teaching, heartfelt worship, and meaningful mission outreach — all aimed at raising up individuals who are spiritually empowered, socially equipped, and mentally engaged.

Our Foundation

What We Believe

"We believe that through sound biblically based preaching and teaching, heartfelt worship, and meaningful mission outreach, we can illuminate God's light of transformation; thereby raising up individuals who are spiritually empowered, socially equipped, and mentally engaged."

As Paul declared in Romans 12:2 — "Be not conformed to this world: but be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind, that ye may prove what is that good, and acceptable, and perfect, will of God."

How We Live It Out

The Three Pillars of The TAB

Sound Teaching

Biblically grounded preaching and teaching that is relevant, challenging, and life-changing — every week.

Heartfelt Worship

Worship that moves beyond form and into the presence of God — authentic, diverse, and transforming.

Mission Outreach

Taking the light of the Gospel beyond the church walls and into every corner of South Mississippi.
