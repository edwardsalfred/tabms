Our Ministries

A ministry for every season of life — 13+ ways to connect, serve, and grow.

More Than a Sunday

Life-Changing Community
All Week Long

Whether you're looking to serve the community, build relationships, or find a space to grow in your faith, The TAB has a ministry home for you. Explore what's available and reach out to get connected.

Ministers

The ordained leadership team serving the congregation and community in pastoral care and teaching.

Music Ministry

Anointed worship that lifts the spirit and ushers the congregation into the presence of God.

GENex

The next generation of leaders — youth and young adults rooted in faith, purpose, and community impact.

Real Ladies of TAB

Uplifting, empowering, and equipping women to discover and walk boldly in their God-given purpose.

Men's Ministry

Building men of God who lead their families, communities, and the church with integrity and strength.

Manna Ministry

Nourishing the congregation and community — both spiritually and physically — in the spirit of God's provision.

Outreach

Carrying the mission of The TAB beyond the church walls and into the heart of the D'Iberville community.

Zelok Care

Caring ministry providing support, compassion, and practical help for those in need within our community.

Media Ministry

Capturing and broadcasting the message of The TAB — from live streaming to social media and beyond.

Deacons

The backbone of service within the congregation — supporting the pastor and ministering to the needs of the body.

Trustees

Faithful stewards of the church's resources, facilities, and finances — protecting the mission for generations to come.

Ushers Ministry

The welcoming face of The TAB — creating an atmosphere of warmth, order, and hospitality every service.

Culinary Ministry

Serving with love through food — from fellowship meals to community events, the culinary team feeds body and soul.

SWAAG

Spiritually Whole, Actively Advancing God's Glory — young men stepping into purpose and identity in Christ.

Special Touch

A ministry of care and compassion dedicated to those who need a special touch from the body of Christ.

And It Came to Pass

A ministry centered on testimony and the faithfulness of God — celebrating what He has done in the lives of our members.

The Link

Connecting the generations — building bridges between young and old so that wisdom and energy flow in both directions.
