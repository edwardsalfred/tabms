## Technical Observations

- Built on Wix website builder
- Uses Realm (onrealm.org / ACST) for online giving — good integration
- Images hosted on wixstatic.com CDN
- Many ministry card images appear broken (showing empty-state placeholders)
- Site has no animation beyond basic Wix transitions
- Mobile-responsive (Wix handles this automatically)
- No video on homepage (static images only)
- CSS uses Wix CSS variable system (--color_XX) — not accessible for customization without Wix editor

---

## Brand Gaps & Opportunities

1. **Identity Crisis** — "My Site" title, broken FB links, Canva logo: signals an unpolished brand
2. **No video content** — top competitors all use video heroes; The TAB has strong photo assets
3. **Ministry grid broken** — image placeholders undermine the depth of what they offer (13+ ministries!)
4. **No testimonials/social proof** — no member stories, no attendance numbers, no community impact data
5. **Weak CTA strategy** — "More About Us Here!" is not compelling; no clear next step for new visitors
6. **No events prominence** — service times buried, no dynamic events calendar on homepage
7. **SEO zero** — "My Site" title, no schema, no meta descriptions = invisible to Google
